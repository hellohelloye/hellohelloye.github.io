RxPermission is an elegant Android acquire permissions tool. I think RxPermission is brilliant.
Lately, I was trying to implement the RxPermission library followed by your GitHub repository.
But I met quite a few issue, I hope my suggestion would be convenient for more developers. I realize it mentioned RxJava, but I think it is better also put RxJava dependences library together with rxpermissions:
compile 'com.tbruyelle.rxpermissions:rxpermissions:0.9.3@aar'
compile 'io.reactivex:rxjava:1.2.2'
The missing of rxjava dependence took me a little bit while to figure it out. The second issue is quite important about your sample code. The reason I am applying RxPermission library is to expect a more straightforward result about a default pop-up window from google to ask permission from user.
But from your sample code, I found you create your own customer pop-up window to enable the rxPermission.request permission function. I think this approach is very silly.
I think the best way to apply rxpermission with default google permission acquire window would be connected with manifests
uses-permission android:name="android.permission.RECORD_AUDIO"as example.
